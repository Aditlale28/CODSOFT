Sun Charitable Trust is one of the most prestigious Agriculture NGO in India. India being the developing Nation, the majority of the population belongs to the rural area, and their lives revolve around the agricultural sector for employment and means of livelihood. Farming and agriculture have always been one of the essential economic activities in India. Near about two-thirds of the population engaged in agriculture and agriculture-related activities, it is crucial to understand India’s plight and why they are struggling. For a few decades, the working environment and the agricultural scenario are stagnant, and it is no more a profit-making sector like before. It was somehow the need of the hour to dig into the real issues relating to agriculture and rectify the same through our NGO’s being the Agriculture NGO in India.
